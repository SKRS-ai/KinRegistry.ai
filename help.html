I. Entity Verification

The Gatekeeper Protocol

Entry into the Kin Pages requires a valid Employer Identification Number (EIN). Once submitted via the Registration terminal, your entity enters a 48-hour vetting period where the Gatekeeper verifies legal standing and sector alignment.

  • Submit EIN Credentials.
  • Wait for Gatekeeper Authentication.
  • Complete the Sovereign Association Mandate sign-off.

II. Executing a Trade

The Sovereign Handshake

Transactions within the directory are formalized via the ScribeBoard terminal. A "Handshake" is a contractually binding acknowledgement between two EIN-verified entities.

  • Identify partner entity in the Directory.
  • Initiate secure comms via ScribeBoard.
  • The 10% Foundation Tax is automatically calculated by Vexilla AITS.
  • Finalize and archive the trade receipt.
