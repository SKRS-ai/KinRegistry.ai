TRI-STATE LEGACY // DIGITAL SOVEREIGNTY

OUR GENESIS

Kin Pages is the digital evolution of the Tri-State Minority Yellow Pages—the premier African American directory of minority businesses and services. For decades, we served as the vital link between minority entrepreneurs and the communities that sustain them.

"Your link to minority businesses and services, bridging the gap between physical heritage and digital trade."

THE MISSION

In 2026, we have transitioned this archive into a sovereign digital ecosystem. Kin Pages is no longer just a list; it is a multicultural assembly. We provide a protected environment where trade, canvas art, and community development intersect under the Vexilla AITS security protocols.

THE 10% FOUNDATION MANDATE

Every transaction within the Kin Pages network—from Canvas Art acquisitions to premium classified listings—contributes 10% to the Koinonia Grace Foundation. This fund is dedicated to "Think Team Not Gang" initiatives, providing resources for the next generation of multicultural builders.

IDENTITY & INCLUSION

We recognize the vast spectrum of multicultural identity. Our directory and assembly are designed to honor every cultural hub and every individual identity, including the full range of gender expressions and pronouns. We are a "Team" in the truest sense.
