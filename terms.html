Membership is granted based on the selected Tier (Artisan, Studio, Gallery, Masterpiece). Dues are non-refundable and cover the maintenance of the Vexilla AITS Master Portal and the promotion of the Associate's profile within the global directory.

Associates retain full ownership of their Intellectual Property. However, by listing on the Master Scroll, you grant the Gatekeeper a license to display said works for promotional and directory purposes within the Solomon Kin ecosystem.
