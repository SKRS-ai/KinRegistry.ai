THE TRI-STATE LEGACY

Established in 1999, the Tri-State Minority Yellow Pages served as the primary physical directory for African American and minority-owned businesses in the Pennsylvania, New Jersey, and Delaware regions. Kin Pages acts as the digital continuation of this mission, preserving the heritage while enabling global trade.

MERCHANT VERIFICATION (EIN)

To maintain the integrity of the Assembly, all Pro Merchants must provide a valid Employer Identification Number (EIN). This process ensures that "Kin Pages" remains a network of legitimate entities, protecting both the buyer and the seller within our sovereign economy.

THE 10% MANDATE OVERVIEW

The Koinonia Grace Foundation operates under a strict mandate: 10% of all gross platform revenue is diverted to the Fellowship fund. These resources are allocated toward "Think Team Not Gang" initiatives, focusing on coding bootcamps, trade certification, and youth entrepreneurship.
